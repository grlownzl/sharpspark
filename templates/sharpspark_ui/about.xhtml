About Sharp Spark

Sharp Spark is the Brain child of Victoria Low, a talented and driven teaching professional

Sharp Sparks allows students from the age of 7 upwards to learn programming skills. The aim is to give each and every child skills that last a lifetime as well as set them up in an ever-changing technical world. The courses focusing on the problem solving, computer program languages and manipulation of already created programs. We are passionate about learning and removing the barriers that prevent the thought that all can achieve.

Starting in August 2013 there will be:

  • 2 day Holiday programs (10 hours)
  • Weekly sessions at a local venue
  • Weekend Courses

Individualised program for schools and Gifted and talented programs are also offered
